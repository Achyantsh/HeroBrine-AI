# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

# HeroBrine AI

## Vision

HeroBrine AI is an AI-powered Commitment Intelligence Platform that automatically discovers commitments from emails, documents, screenshots, conversations, and other sources, then helps users plan and execute them before deadlines are missed.

---

## Problem Statement

People receive commitments across multiple platforms.

Examples include:

- Emails
- PDFs
- WhatsApp
- Google Docs
- Calendar
- Meeting notes

Most productivity apps require manual task creation.

If users forget to create a task, they receive no reminder.

---

## Solution

HeroBrine AI automatically detects commitments using AI.

Every commitment becomes a structured object containing:

- deadline
- priority
- estimated effort
- execution plan
- risk score

Users simply approve AI suggestions.

---

## Target Users

- Students

- Freelancers
- Researchers
